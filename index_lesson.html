Learn - Lesson

Learn from us and your teachers

The lessons that are present within your app is made by us and your teachers who registered to our app and added you to their list of players who can access the lesson that they have made.

Our app's primary focus is on the periodic table, offering in-depth lessons and interactive resources related to this fundamental aspect of chemistry. Through these lessons, you will gain a comprehensive understanding of the elements, their properties, and their placement within the periodic table. We aim to provide a structured and engaging learning experience in the field of chemistry, making it easier for both educators and students to explore and master this subject.

Teachers can upload lessons and invite specific students.

By having teachers who can upload lessons, Elementum can provide a flexible and personalized environment for students in order to for them to learn as much as they can when learning about the periodic table.

How does it work?

  • Upload your lesson file

    By clicking the upload button on this page or your account page, you will be directed to another page where you can upload your lesson files. These files should meet our page's requirements, including the following: File extensions limited to pdf, ppt, and doc formats. A maximum file size of 100MB. Inclusion of the word "Elementum" in the file title for acceptance.

  • File uploaded will appear in our app

    The file that you uploaded will now go to our app and be seen by everyone and they are free to read it as they please.

  • Read the lessons and enjoy

    Creating and sharing your personalized lessons on Elementum allows them to be accessed and studied by numerous users. You have the freedom to manage and delete your uploaded lessons as you see fit.

Consider using one of our recommended templates for you to create your own lesson.
