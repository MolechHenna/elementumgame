Experiments

Experiment and discover

Discover many compounds by combining two different elements. The possibilities are endless, as long as it exists.

Discover countless compounds that can be created from basic elements.

How does it work?

  • Choose two elements

    Choose any two unique elements from the periodic element and you will see those element inside the mixing flask.

  • Create compounds

    By clicking mix, you will see all of the possible compounds that can be created with those two elements.

  • See compound Information

    By clicking one of the compounds created, you can see information regarding that compound.
